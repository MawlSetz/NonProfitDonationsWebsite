We're filling the
transportation gap

Everyone Rides Nice is a non-profit, 501(c)3 agency that provides bus fare to those in need. NICE provides the service while ERN provides the fare. ERN provides local agencies with free bus transportation in the form of MetroCards. Everyone Rides NICE is a joint effort of Nassau County and its transportation partner, TransdevNA, which is also the operator of the Nassau Inter-County Express, commonly called NICE. ERN is an affiliate of United Way of Long Island.

For many of our fellow citizens with limited resources the cost of a round-trip bus journey represents a significant drain on those resources and may require trading off transportation for other essentials like paying a bill, securing food or medical care or a day’s child care. ERN fills the transportation gap by providing free bus transportation in the form of single-ride MetroCards usable on NICE buses. By working exclusively through social service agencies already in touch with clients who benefit from help with transportation costs, ERN also supports those agencies in the performance of their mission by providing mobility for their clients at no cost to the agencies. ERN receives no tax funding and depends on the generosity of the community.

FAQs

Can I get free MetroCards directly from ERN?

No, ERN works exclusively through agencies that are already in touch with people who need the transportation assistance. A list of agencies currently participating in ERN can be found by clicking here

How will ERN decide if I am eligible?

Eligibility decisions are made by the participating agencies, not by ERN.

Will ERN assist me with LIRR or taxi fare?

No ERN is only able to provide MetroCards usable on NICE buses. Can I transfer to a subway or bus operated by MTA or Suffolk Transit. No, ERN provided Metro Cards are good for a journey on NICE only, though they do provide free transfer priveleges from one NICE bus to another NICE bus.

Where does ERN get its funds?

ERN began with a $1.25 million grant from Transdev and actively seeks funding from private philanthropists, other foundations, and other sources. Please consider including ERN in your generosity.

How does ERN choose the agencies it works with?

There is an application process for agencies and specific criteria. Agencies interested in participating should contact ERN by clicking on this link

Is there a connection between ERN and the United Way of Long Island?

ERN contracts with United Way of LI for administrative services. UWLI assures donors that their generaosity goes directly to work for those in need of such help.

Can I contribute to ERN?

Yes, please help out if you possibly can. Contribution of all sizes are the lifeblood of the organization. ERN is a 501(c)3 organization so your contributions are fully deductible for tax purposes.
