Everyone Rides Nice is a non-profit that provides bus fare to those in need.
NICE bus provides the service, while ERN provides the fare.


We're working to
fill the transportation gap



for people who need access to opportunity. People who get vital support from non-profit agencies can get the foot-up they need.

ERN fills the transportation gap by providing free bus rides

For many of our fellow citizens with limited resources the cost of a round-trip bus journey represents a significant drain on those resources and may require trading off transportation for other essentials like paying a bill, securing food or medical care or a day’s child care. ERN fills the transportation gap by providing free bus transportation in the form of MetroCards usable on NICE buses with transfer privileges to and from MTA service in Queens.
