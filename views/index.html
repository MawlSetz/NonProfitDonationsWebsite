Everyone Rides Nice fills the transportation gap for people who need access to opportunity. Job-seekers, learners, and people who get vital support from non-profit agencies can’t enrich their lives unless they can get to and from those opportunities. NICE provides the service while ERN provides the fare.

Sometimes all people need
is a bus ride

ERN exists to provide access to things and services that are vital to Long Islanders’ well-being. Sometimes all people need is a bus ride to end their isolation and enrich their lives. Everyone Rides NICE makes sure that the cost of bus fare is not a barrier to those people.

ERN fills the transportation gap by providing free bus rides

For many of our fellow citizens with limited resources the cost of a round-trip bus journey represents a significant drain on those resources and may require trading off transportation for other essentials like paying a bill, securing food or medical care or a day’s child care. ERN fills the transportation gap by providing free bus transportation in the form of MetroCards usable on NICE buses with transfer privileges to and from MTA service in Queens.
